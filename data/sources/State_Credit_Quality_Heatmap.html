U.S. Credit Quality & Financed-Purchase Affordability by State

Screening view for market-based territory sizing. Built entirely from public data: Experian average FICO® Score by state (September 2025) and U.S. Census Bureau median household income by state (2024). Hover any state for the full profile.
Reference affordability case: $8,500 package financed at 24.99% APR (top of U.S. Bank Avvance’s published 0%–24.99% range) over 60 months → $249.44/month. At a conservative 8% payment-to-income screen, required household income ≈ $37,415. The affordability multiple in the tooltips = state median income ÷ $37,415. Assumptions are editable in the companion Excel model.
